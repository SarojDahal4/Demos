The Very Best Australian Seafood
Fresh Australian Seafood is Searock Grill’s specialty. So fresh in fact, we fly our West Australian lobsters in daily, so you only get the best. Lobsters are the mouth-watering hero in our ‘indulgence platter’, which also includes: Crystal Bay prawns, Sydney rock oysters, Tasmanian black mussels, Blue Swimmer crabs and Moreton Bay bugs.We also serve fresh fish, Salmon, Barramundi and fish & chips
